CARGO SKIRT

Cut + Sewn by Diego Allison, Dyed by Abbey Lehr

After cutting up and dyeing two sets of cargo pants, we began to imagine the new uses of the chunky pockets. By sewing together two pieces we created a front panel of what we intend to be a skirt. We incorporated the striped fabric from another project to add elements of a shirt. We see this piece moving forward as a skirt decked out with many additional finishes such elements such as zippers, clasps, etc.
